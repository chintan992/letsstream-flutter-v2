# Let's Stream Logo Design Specification

## Primary Logo
- **Shape**: Rounded square (64x64 pixels minimum)
- **Background**: Dark charcoal gray (#1A1A1A)
- **Primary Element**: Purple play button (#6200EE) with white arrow
- **Accent Elements**: Three vertical streaming waves in decreasing opacity (30%, 20%, 10%)
- **Text**: "Let's Stream" in white Roboto Bold, size proportional to logo

## App Icon
- **Shape**: Rounded square with 24px corner radius
- **Background**: Dark charcoal gray (#1A1A1A)
- **Primary Element**: Purple play button (#6200EE) with white arrow
- **Accent Elements**: Three small vertical bars representing streaming waves

## Color Palette
- Primary Purple: #6200EE
- Dark Background: #1A1A1A
- White Text: #FFFFFF
- Streaming Wave Opacities: 30%, 20%, 10%

## Design Elements
1. Play button represents media playback
2. Vertical bars represent streaming/data flow
3. Modern, clean aesthetic
4. Good contrast for accessibility
5. Scalable design that works from 48px to 1024px

## Required Sizes
- 48x48px - Notification icons
- 72x72px - Launcher icons (MDPI)
- 96x96px - Launcher icons (HDPI)
- 144x144px - Launcher icons (XHDPI)
- 192x192px - Launcher icons (XXHDPI)
- 512x512px - High-res marketing
- 1024x1024px - App store listings

This design represents a modern streaming service with:
- Play button symbolizing media playback
- Streaming waves representing data flow
- Clean, accessible color scheme
- Scalable vector-based design
