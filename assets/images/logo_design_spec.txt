# Let's Stream Logo Design Specification (v2)

## Primary Logo
- **Shape**: Stylized 'S' play button.
- **Color**: Gradient from blue (#007BFF) to purple (#8A2BE2).
- **Background**: Dark grey (#121212).
- **Text**: "Let's Stream" in white Roboto Bold.

## App Icon
- **Shape**: Rounded square.
- **Background**: Dark grey (#121212).
- **Primary Element**: Stylized 'S' play button with a blue-to-purple gradient.

## Color Palette
- Primary Gradient: Blue (#007BFF) to Purple (#8A2BE2)
- Dark Background: #121212
- White Text: #FFFFFF
